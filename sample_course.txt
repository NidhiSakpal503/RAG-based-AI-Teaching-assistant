Course: Introduction to Data Structures

Lecture 1: Arrays and Linked Lists
- Arrays are contiguous memory structures.
- Linked Lists are composed of nodes with pointers to next elements.

Lecture 2: Stacks and Queues
- Stack is LIFO; push and pop operations.
- Queue is FIFO; enqueue and dequeue operations.

Lecture 3: Trees
- Binary Tree: at most two children per node.
- Binary Search Tree: left < node < right property.
